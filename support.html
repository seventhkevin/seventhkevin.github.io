Timer4Class Support

Welcome to the support page for Timer4Class, a watchOS app designed for Pilates instructors to manage class sessions with a countdown timer, notifications, and haptic feedback.

Getting Started

  • Install the App: Download Timer4Class from the App Store on your Apple Watch.
  • Start a Timer: Open the app, select a duration (e.g., 50 minutes), and the countdown will begin.
  • Notifications & Haptics: The app will notify you with haptics at 5 minutes (5 taps), 2 minutes (2 taps), and when the timer ends (success haptic). Notifications also appear as banners.
  • Stop the Timer: Tap the gray "X" button in the top-left corner to stop the timer and return to the start screen.

Troubleshooting

  • Notifications Not Firing: Ensure notifications are enabled for Timer4Class in your watch’s Settings app (Settings > Notifications > Timer4Class).
  • Haptics Not Working: Haptics play in the foreground. In the background (wrist-down), you’ll feel a default haptic due to watchOS limitations.
  • Timer Resets: If the app is force-quit or the watch reboots, the timer will reset. This is expected in version 1.
